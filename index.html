FAQ

Questions people usually ask.

What does BBD do?

BBD does operations and AI advisory for 5–50 person teams that are stuck — broken handoffs, tools that don't work the way the team needs, or ideas that won't turn into action. BBD works hands-on, not as a typical consultant: getting into the systems, fixing the process, and making sure it sticks.

Is BBD a fractional COO?

Close, but more hands-on. BBD brings the same operational judgment as a fractional COO — diagnosing what's broken, owning the fix, working across departments — but also does the work itself: building the systems, configuring the tools, writing the playbook. Think fractional COO+ — strategy and execution from the same place.

How is BBD different from a consultant or agency?

Consultants typically hand you a deck and leave. BBD works inside your tools — Salesforce, HubSpot, Outreach, whatever your stack is — and builds the fix alongside your team. No big team, no markup, no months-long discovery phase. You get a senior operator who's actually done the work, not someone managing the people who do.

What kind of problems does BBD solve?

Three areas: making your business systems actually work (tech stack, AI, customer journey), building customer success functions that scale beyond SaaS, and turning stalled ideas into executed plans.

When should you bring BBD in?

When you're the bottleneck — every decision routes through you — or when you're firefighting instead of building, and good ideas keep stalling for lack of structure. If your tools and your team aren't talking to each other, that's usually the sign.

How much does it cost to work with BBD?

Engagements start at $5,000 and scale from there based on scope — a focused project sprint costs less than an ongoing retainer. The structure follows the problem, not a fixed package. The fastest way to get a real number is to talk through what's broken.

How do you start working with BBD?

Email and describe what's not working. No intake form, no sales process. Expect a response within 24 hours, then a conversation to figure out the right engagement.

Does BBD work with companies outside New Orleans?

Yes. Based in New Orleans, working everywhere — most engagements happen remotely.
