Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2027

The short version

This site collects minimal information. I don't sell your data, I don't share it with third parties for marketing purposes, and I only use what I need to respond to you and improve the site.

Who this is

This privacy policy applies to bbdworks.com, operated by Brook Bissinger Davis ("BBD", "I", "me"). If you have questions about this policy, you can reach me at brook@bbdworks.com.

What I collect

When you fill out the contact form on this site, I collect:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Basic business details you choose to share (company name, team size, what you're working on)
  • Any other information you include in your message

I only collect what you voluntarily provide. I don't collect any information automatically through the contact form.

Google Analytics

This site uses Google Analytics to understand how visitors find and use the site. Google Analytics collects anonymous usage data including:

  • Pages visited and time spent on the site
  • General geographic location (country/city level)
  • Device and browser type
  • How you arrived at the site (search, direct, referral)

This data is aggregated and anonymous — I cannot identify you personally through Google Analytics. Google may use this data in accordance with their own privacy policy. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking using the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on.

How I use your information

Information you submit through the contact form is used only to:

  • Respond to your inquiry
  • Follow up on a potential engagement
  • Communicate with you about working together

I do not add you to any mailing list, newsletter, or marketing sequence without your explicit consent.

What I don't do

  • Sell your data to anyone, ever
  • Share your information with third parties for marketing purposes
  • Use your information for any purpose beyond responding to you
  • Store your data in any CRM or marketing platform without telling you

Cookies

This site uses cookies only in connection with Google Analytics. These are analytics cookies that help me understand site traffic. They do not track you across other websites or store personal information.

If you prefer not to accept cookies, you can adjust your browser settings to decline them. Note that this may affect some site functionality.

Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of any personal information I hold about you
  • Ask me to correct or delete your information
  • Withdraw consent for me to contact you at any time

To exercise any of these rights, just email me directly.

Data retention

I retain contact form submissions only as long as needed to follow up on your inquiry. If we don't end up working together, I delete your information within a reasonable timeframe. If you'd like me to delete your information sooner, just ask.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes in any meaningful way, I'll update the date at the top of this page. I won't notify you by email unless the changes are significant.

Questions?

If you have any questions about how your information is handled, just reach out directly. No forms required.
